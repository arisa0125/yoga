About Kaldo



Concept


CALDO means "warm" in Italian.
Cardo was born to warm the hearts and bodies of many people.

The concept is "continue from fun"
We will continue to create entertainment spaces where you can enjoy the dynamics of your body.





Characteristics of Kardo's Hot Yoga

The best of temperature and humidity suitable for the Hot yoga.
Cardo's hot yoga is done at room temperature of 35-40Â°C, which makes it easier to stretch your muscles even when your body is not warm.
The muscle fibers are soft and easy to stretch, which also helps prevent muscle damage during yoga. Humidity is 55%. We maintain an environment where sweating and evaporation of sweat are most appropriate so that the skin does not dry out during yoga while moderately evaporating sweat.





Clean space with silver ion steam

Cardo's hot studio uses the industry's first "silver ion steam", which is a humidifier mixed with silver ion. You can take hot yoga and yoga lessons in a comfortable and clean environment by using silver ions. In addition, the steam is always kept at 55% by mechanical control, and you can feel the amazing sweating action and the moisturization of the skin by the steam.
